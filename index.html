WELCOME SUMMER

Zermatt

Summertime in Zermatt, Switzerland is flush with color. It is when the snowcapped mountains are accented with lush, green meadows and a mesmerizing blue color in the lakes and so on

Seattle

The city of Seattle is known for two things: great coffee and gloomy weather. The summer is high tourist season in this low-key city, so hotel rates are higher but it is a great time to visit.

Marseille

A summer visit to France in the port city of Marseille is one of the best places in the country to visit in the warmer months. The summer climate, especially in June and July, is spectacular

Edinburgh

Summer is festival time in Edinburgh and the height of tourist season, so book travel and hotels far in advance. You can also Walk round the city and take a sightseeing tour on a double bus

Chigago

Walking the Magnificent Mile and spending time in Grant Park. With the sun at its full potential in July and August, you can get a great reflective selfie in "The Bean" sculpture in Millennium Park.

Machu Piccu

Since Machu Piccu is a bucket-list vacation for many people, it is best to experience it in the summer, when there is less chance of rain showers and inclement weather, especially if you plan to hike.

The islands of the Maldives are best experienced in the summertime when they have dry weather.
